We're delighted you're considering BIC Community Preschool for your child's early education journey. Our school has been serving families in the community for years, providing a safe, loving, and Christ-centered environment where children can thrive.

Our dedicated team of educators is committed to nurturing each child's unique gifts and helping them develop a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Our Story

Our History

Learn about our journey and how we've grown to serve our community.

Founded on Faith and Community

BIC Community Preschool was established with a vision to provide exceptional early childhood education rooted in Christian values. Over the years, we have grown from a small program to a comprehensive preschool and kindergarten serving families throughout our community.

Our commitment to excellence in education, combined with our focus on spiritual development, has made us a trusted choice for families seeking a nurturing environment for their children.

Today, we continue to honor our founding principles while embracing innovative teaching methods that prepare children for success in school and in life.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

The driving force behind everything we do at BIC Community Preschool.

Our mission in action

Nurturing Minds, Touching Hearts

Our mission is to provide a high-quality, Christ-centered early childhood education that nurtures the whole child—spiritually, academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

We believe every child is a unique creation with God-given potential. Our goal is to partner with families to help each child develop a love for learning, a strong moral foundation, and the confidence to succeed.

Through play-based learning, intentional teaching, and a supportive community, we prepare children not just for kindergarten, but for a lifetime of learning and growth.

Our Foundation

Church Affiliation

Learn about our connection to BIC Church and our shared mission.

Rooted in Faith

BIC Community Preschool is a ministry of BIC Church, reflecting our shared commitment to serving families in our community. Our church has been a pillar of the community for decades, providing spiritual guidance, support, and outreach programs.

As a church-affiliated preschool, we incorporate biblical teachings into our daily activities, including weekly chapel services where children learn age-appropriate Bible stories, songs, and verses. This spiritual foundation complements our academic curriculum, helping children develop strong character and moral values.

We welcome families of all faith backgrounds and strive to create an inclusive environment where every child feels loved and valued.
