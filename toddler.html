Toddler Program

Our Toddler Program is designed for children who are eager to explore, discover, and become more independent. This exciting stage of development is filled with curiosity, and our teachers provide a structured yet playful environment where toddlers can safely learn through hands-on experiences.

Our Approach

Nurturing Curiosity

Our Toddler Program nurtures children eager to explore, discover, and grow. Our teachers create a structured, playful environment where toddlers learn through hands-on experiences.

Children participate in activities that encourage language development, early problem-solving, creativity, movement, and social interaction. As they gain confidence, toddlers begin developing self-help skills and routines that prepare them for preschool. Along with parents, teachers will assist with toilet training.

What We Offer

Program Highlights

Key features of our toddler program.

Hands-on Learning Through Play

Interactive activities that engage children's natural curiosity and love of discovery.

Language & Vocabulary Development

Story time, conversations, and vocabulary activities that support early language development.

Music, Movement & Creative Art

Singing, dancing, rhythm activities, and art exploration that build coordination and self-expression.

Social & Emotional Growth

Guided interactions that help toddlers learn to share, communicate, and build friendships.

Fine & Gross Motor Development

Activities that develop both fine motor skills (grasping, stacking) and gross motor skills (walking, climbing).

Independence & Self-Help Skills

Encouragement of independence and self-help skills to prepare children for preschool.

Nurturing curious minds through play-based learning, community connection, and joyful early childhood education.
