Summer Camp at B.I.C. Community Preschool

A Summer Full of Learning, Friendship & Fun!

Give your child a summer filled with exciting adventures, hands-on learning, and unforgettable memories! Our Summer Camp is designed for children 12 months through Kindergarten and provides a safe, nurturing environment where children can explore, create, play, and grow.

Each week features engaging, age-appropriate activities that encourage creativity, curiosity, and confidence while helping children continue learning throughout the summer.

Our Summer Camp Includes:

  • Weekly themed adventures
  • Arts & crafts
  • Water play days
  • Outdoor games and playground fun
  • Music and movement
  • Science and sensory exploration
  • Story time and literacy activities
  • STEM discovery projects
  • Dramatic play and imaginative learning
  • Fine and gross motor development
  • Kindergarten readiness activities for our older campers

Programs Available

Infant & Toddler Camp (12–24 Months)

Our youngest campers enjoy a loving, nurturing environment with sensory play, music, movement, outdoor exploration, and activities designed to support early development while maintaining familiar daily routines.

Preschool Camp (2–5 Years)

Children participate in exciting weekly themes filled with hands-on learning, creative projects, outdoor adventures, and social experiences that keep young minds active and engaged all summer long.

Kindergarten Readiness Camp

Perfect for children entering Kindergarten, this program combines fun summer activities with early literacy, math, science, problem-solving, and social-emotional learning to help children feel confident and prepared for the school year ahead.

Why Families Choose Our Summer Camp

  • Safe, caring, and licensed preschool environment
  • Experienced and nurturing teachers
  • Small class sizes
  • Age-appropriate curriculum and activities
  • Opportunities to build friendships and confidence
  • A balance of structured learning and creative play

Join us for a summer filled with laughter, learning, exploration, and fun! Spaces are limited, so enroll early to reserve your child's spot.
