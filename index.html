Enrichment Activities

At B.I.C. Community Preschool, we believe children learn best through hands-on experiences, exploration, and play. Our enrichment activities are thoughtfully designed to support every child's social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development while making learning fun and engaging.

Our enrichment opportunities may include:

Music & Movement

Singing, dancing, rhythm instruments, and creative movement that build coordination and self-expression.

Art & Creativity

Painting, drawing, crafts, and sensory art experiences that encourage imagination and fine motor development.

STEM Exploration

Age-appropriate science, technology, engineering, and math activities that inspire curiosity and problem-solving.

Outdoor Learning

Nature exploration, gardening, gross motor play, and outdoor discovery that promote healthy development.

Sensory Play

Engaging experiences with textures, water, sand, and other sensory materials to encourage exploration and learning.

Literacy & Story Time

Interactive reading, storytelling, phonics, and language activities that foster a lifelong love of books.

Cooking & Nutrition

Simple cooking activities that introduce healthy foods, following directions, and teamwork.

Dramatic Play

Imaginative play centers that develop communication, creativity, and social skills.

Kindergarten Readiness

Activities that build early literacy, math, critical thinking, independence, and confidence to prepare children for a successful transition to kindergarten.

Special Events & Celebrations

Seasonal activities, community helpers, family events, themed learning days, and special guests that enrich the preschool experience.

Amazing Athletes

Multisport Program which helps set kids on a path to an active and healthy lifestyle.

Bible Based Curriculum and Weekly Chapels

Designed for age-appropriate learning, children learn bible stories, praise/worship and learn simple bible verses.

Every enrichment activity is planned with each child's developmental stage in mind, creating meaningful opportunities to learn, discover, and grow in a safe, nurturing, and Christ-centered environment.

Our Promise

Why Families Choose B.I.C. Community Preschool

At B.I.C. Community Preschool, we believe every child is uniquely created with incredible potential. Our experienced, educated and background checked staff provides a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment where children feel loved, valued, and encouraged every day.

From infancy through kindergarten, we partner with families to foster each child's academic, social, emotional, and physical development. Through faith, learning, and play, we build a strong foundation that prepares children for success in school and in life.

We are committed to helping every child grow with confidence, kindness, curiosity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Designed for Every Stage of Childhood

Our programs embrace how children learn best — through curiosity, connection, and joyful exploration tailored to each age group.

Toddler Care

Warm, responsive care during the most sensitive developmental window. We build trust, attachment, and early language skills through gentle daily routines.

Preschool Program

Kindergarten
