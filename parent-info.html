Getting Started

Enrollment Process

Joining our family is simple. Here's how to get started.

1. Schedule a Tour

Visit our school to see our classrooms, meet our teachers, and experience our learning environment firsthand. Tours are available by appointment.

2. Complete Application

Fill out our enrollment application form with your child's information, including emergency contacts, medical information, and developmental history.

3. Submit Documents

Provide required documents including birth certificate, immunization records, and proof of residency. Our team will guide you through the process.

4. Orientation

Attend our family orientation to learn about our policies, daily routines, and how to partner with us for your child's success.

Guidelines

School Policies

Our policies ensure a safe, nurturing environment for all children.

Health & Safety

We maintain strict health and safety protocols including regular cleaning, secure entry systems, and health screening procedures to protect all children.

Attendance

Regular attendance helps children build routines and relationships. Please notify us if your child will be absent due to illness or family circumstances.

Communication

We use multiple channels to keep you informed including daily reports, monthly newsletters, parent-teacher conferences, and our parent portal.

Tuition & Payments

Tuition is due monthly with flexible payment options available. We offer sibling discounts and financial assistance for qualifying families.
