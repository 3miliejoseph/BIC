Our Approach

Kindergarten Program

Our Kindergarten Program provides a strong academic foundation while nurturing each child's confidence, character, and love of learning.

A Strong Foundation

Brethren in Christ offers small classroom sizes where teachers are able to provide children with individualized and personal attention that they need. We combine engaging instruction with hands-on activities that promote growth in reading, writing, mathematics, science, social studies, and creative enrichment.

Students develop critical thinking, collaboration, leadership, and independence in a caring classroom environment where they are encouraged to reach their full potential.

What We Offer

Program Highlights

Key features of our kindergarten program.

Comprehensive Academic Curriculum

Reading, writing, and mathematics alongside science and social studies for a well-rounded education.

Hands-On Learning

Engaging, hands-on learning experiences that bring the curriculum to life through exploration and discovery.

Art, Music & PE

Creative enrichment through art, music, and physical education supports the whole child's growth.

Character Development

Intentional focus on building confidence, kindness, and leadership for lifelong learning.

Personalized Attention

Small class sizes with a degreed teacher ensuring every child receives individualized support.

Award-Winning Abeka Curriculum

Preparation for lifelong learning through the proven, nationally recognized Abeka curriculum.

Nurturing curious minds through play-based learning, community connection, and joyful early childhood education.
